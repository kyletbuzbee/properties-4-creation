About Properties 4 Creations

Building homes, rebuilding lives - One veteran at a time

Our Mission

Properties 4 Creations exists to honor the service of veterans and families by providing premium, renovated housing that serves as the foundation for rebuilding lives and strengthening communities across East Texas.

Founded and operated by veterans, we understand the unique challenges our fellow service members face when transitioning to civilian life. Stable, quality housing isn't just a need‚Äîit's a stepping stone to success.

Our Story

2020

The Beginning

After witnessing too many veterans struggling with housing stability, our founder decided to take action. Properties 4 Creations was established with a single mission: provide quality housing for those who served.

2021

First Renovations

We purchased and renovated our first property in Tyler, Texas. Every home had to meet our rigorous standards - no shortcuts, no compromises. The just before first family moved in Christmas.

2022

Growth & Partnerships

We partnered with the VA and local housing authorities to become HUD-VASH and Section 8 certified. Our reputation for quality and veteran-focused service began to grow across East Texas.

2023

Expansion

We expanded operations to Marshall, Mineola, and Longview. Our portfolio grew to include townhomes, historic properties, and modern studios - each renovated with the same attention to detail and veteran-first approach.

2024

Recognition

Properties 4 Creations received recognition from the Texas Veterans Commission for our outstanding service to the veteran community. We continue to grow while maintaining our core values and commitment to excellence.
