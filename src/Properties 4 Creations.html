üéñÔ∏è HUD-VASH (Veterans Affairs Supportive Housing)

A collaborative program between the VA and HUD providing rental assistance and supportive services to homeless veterans and their families.

  • Eligibility: Must be a veteran with a housing crisis due to homelessness or at-risk homelessness
  • Income Requirement: Income at or below 50% of Area Median Income (AMI)
  • Our Role: We accept HUD-VASH vouchers and provide quality housing without discrimination
  • Process: Veterans are referred by the VA after receiving a voucher

üè† Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers

The most common rental assistance program for very low-income families, elderly, and disabled persons.

  • Eligibility: Income at or below 50% of Area Median Income (AMI)
  • East Texas Example: For a single person in Longview/Tyler, max income ‚âà $19,750/year
  • Our Acceptance: 100% Section 8 acceptance - we welcome voucher holders
  • Timeline: Waitlists vary by housing authority; some are currently closed

üè¶ VA Home Loans

A zero-down-payment mortgage program exclusively for eligible veterans, active duty service members, and surviving spouses.

  • Benefits: No down payment required, no PMI, competitive interest rates
  • Texas VA Loans: Up to $806,000 or more depending on county
  • Eligibility: Honorable discharge, active duty 90+ days (or reserves/guard 6+ years)
  • Our Support: We provide financing resources and can guide veteran homebuyers

üìä SSVF (Supportive Services for Veteran Families)

Case management and financial assistance for veterans and families at risk of homelessness or experiencing homelessness.

  • Services: Emergency financial assistance, rental assistance, case management
  • Coverage: Available through VA partners in East Texas
  • Access: Contact your local VA office for eligibility and enrollment

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a property? ‚ñº

Click the "Apply Now" button at the top of the page to start your application. You'll fill out a simple form with your personal information, voucher type, and household details. We'll review your application and contact you within 24-48 hours.

Do you accept Section 8 vouchers? ‚ñº

Yes, we accept 100% Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers. We welcome all eligible applicants with active vouchers from any housing authority. There is no discrimination, and we make the process simple and respectful.

What about HUD-VASH for veterans? ‚ñº

We are HUD-VASH approved and actively work with the VA to house veterans. If you have a HUD-VASH voucher or are interested in the program, we can help guide you through the process and match you with an appropriate property.

What areas do you serve? ‚ñº

We serve East Texas communities including Tyler, Longview, and surrounding areas. Our properties are strategically located to provide access to employment, schools, healthcare, and public transportation.

Are all homes recently renovated? ‚ñº

Yes! Every property in our portfolio meets our rigorous renovation standards. We guarantee new or recently serviced HVAC, roofing, electrical, plumbing, and interior finishes. All work is inspected and warrantied.

What is your company background? ‚ñº

Properties 4 Creations is veteran-owned and operated. We were founded with a mission to provide safe, dignified housing for veterans and families in East Texas while rebuilding communities one home at a time.

How long does the application process take? ‚ñº

Most applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Once we review your information, we'll contact you directly to discuss available properties, answer questions, and move forward with scheduling a viewing if you're a good fit.
