Our Mission

Properties 4 Creations exists to honor the service of veterans and families by providing premium, renovated housing that serves as the foundation for rebuilding lives and strengthening communities across East Texas.

Our Story

2020

The Beginning

After witnessing too many veterans struggling with housing stability, our founder decided to take action. Properties 4 Creations was established with a single mission: provide quality housing for those who served.

2021

First Renovations

We purchased and renovated our first property in Tyler, Texas. Every home had to meet our rigorous standards - no shortcuts, no compromises. The first family moved in just before Christmas.

2022

Growth & Partnerships

We partnered with the VA and local housing authorities to become HUD-VASH and Section 8 certified. Our reputation for quality and veteran-focused service began to grow across East Texas.

2023

Community Impact

As we expanded to Longview and surrounding areas, we began to see the ripple effect: families stabilized, children thriving in school, veterans finding their footing. This became more than a business - it became a calling.

2024

Transparency & Standards

We made a commitment to transparency, publishing our renovation standards and cost breakdowns. If we're going to do this, we do it right - no hiding, no cutting corners.

2025

Today & Beyond

Today, we've housed over 20 veterans and families, renovated 25+ properties, and maintained a 100% voucher acceptance rate. But our journey is just beginning. Every home we create is a chance to rebuild a life.

Our Commitments

Quality First

Every property undergoes comprehensive renovation meeting our $30,000+ investment standards. No shortcuts, no compromises.

  • Full HVAC system replacement
  • Complete roof replacement
  • Modern electrical and plumbing
  • Professional interior finishes

100% Voucher Acceptance

We accept Section 8 and HUD-VASH vouchers without discrimination. Quality housing should be accessible to all.

  • No application fees
  • Non-discriminatory screening
  • Pre-inspected properties
  • Full voucher coordination

Complete Transparency

We publish our standards, costs, and processes. What you see is what you get - no hidden fees or surprises.

  • Published renovation standards
  • Cost breakdown transparency
  • Clear application process
  • Warranty information upfront
