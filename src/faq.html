Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our veteran housing services

You can start your application by clicking the "Apply Now" button on any page or visiting our application page. The process involves:

  1. Completing our online application form with your personal information
  2. Providing details about your housing voucher and income
  3. Allowing 24-48 hours for our team to review your application
  4. Scheduling a property viewing if you're approved
  5. Completing the lease signing process

Our veteran team is here to guide you through each step.

Yes, we accept 100% of Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers. We are committed to helping voucher holders find quality housing without discrimination. Here's what you need to know:

  • We accept vouchers from any housing authority
  • No application fees or discriminatory screening
  • Properties are pre-inspected and Section 8 ready
  • Our team handles all voucher coordination with your housing authority
  • Transparent process with clear communication at every step

If you have questions about voucher acceptance, contact us directly.

We are fully HUD-VASH approved and actively partner with the VA to house veterans. As a veteran-owned company, we understand the unique needs of our fellow service members.

HUD-VASH benefits include:

  • Direct coordination with your VA case manager
  • Veterans receive priority consideration
  • Specialized veteran-friendly properties
  • Ongoing support during the transition to housing
  • Understanding of military culture and experiences

If you're working with the VA on housing, we can help streamline the process.

We serve communities across East Texas, focusing on areas with strong veteran populations and good access to services:

  • Primary Markets: Tyler, Longview
  • Secondary Markets: Marshall, Jefferson, Mineola, Kemp
  • Surrounding Areas: Nacogdoches, Palestine, Kilgore, White Oak

Our properties are strategically located near:

  • VA medical centers and outpatient clinics
  • Public transportation routes
  • Employment opportunities and job training centers
  • Schools and community services
  • Grocery stores and essential services

We're veteran-owned and operated, which means we bring a unique understanding to housing for veterans and families. Here's what sets us apart:

  • 100% Voucher Acceptance: We welcome Section 8 and HUD-VASH without discrimination
  • Premium Renovations: Every property meets our rigorous $30,000+ renovation standards
  • Transparency: We publish our renovation standards and cost breakdowns
  • Quality Guarantees: Comprehensive warranties on all systems
  • Veteran Understanding: We understand military culture and challenges
  • Community Focus: We're invested in rebuilding East Texas communities
  • Fair Housing Compliance: Strict adherence to all anti-discrimination laws

Yes, every property undergoes comprehensive renovation before becoming available. Our renovation process includes:

Core Systems (12-month warranties)

  • Complete HVAC system replacement and certification
  • Full roof replacement with premium materials
  • New plumbing throughout with copper pipes
  • Modern electrical systems with code compliance

Interior Finishes (6-month warranties)

  • Professional painting throughout
  • New or refinished flooring
  • Updated kitchens with new appliances
  • Modern bathrooms with new fixtures
  • Fresh fixtures and lighting

See our complete renovation standards for full details.

The timeline varies based on your voucher status and property availability:

For Applicants with Active Vouchers

  • Application Review: 24-48 hours
  • Document Verification: 1-3 business days
  • Property Viewing: Same week if approved
  • Lease Signing: Within 1-2 weeks

For Applicants Without Vouchers

  • Application Review: 24-48 hours
  • Voucher Guidance: We'll help connect you with local housing authorities
  • Timeline: Depends on housing authority waitlist status

We'll keep you informed throughout the entire process.

We can help guide you through the voucher process. Here's how we assist applicants without vouchers:

Section 8 Vouchers

  • Help you understand eligibility requirements
  • Connect you with local housing authorities
  • Guide you through application processes
  • Monitor waitlist openings

HUD-VASH for Veterans

  • Coordinate with your VA case manager
  • Provide documentation of our veteran-friendly properties
  • Support the voucher application process
  • Expedite property matching once voucher is issued

Contact us to discuss your situation and we'll create a personalized plan.

Income requirements vary by program and are set by the housing authorities, but generally:

Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers

  • Typically 50% or below Area Median Income (AMI)
  • For East Texas (2025): Single person maximum ≈ $19,750/year
  • Four-person household maximum ≈ $28,200/year

HUD-VASH

  • Usually 50% or below AMI
  • Special considerations for homeless veterans
  • Case-by-case evaluation for extreme circumstances

We can help you determine if you meet current income requirements for your area.
