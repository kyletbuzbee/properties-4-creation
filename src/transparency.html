Premium Renovation Standards

Every home we offer meets our rigorous renovation standards. We believe in complete transparency, which is why we're sharing exactly what you get.

HVAC System

  • Certified installation by licensed technicians
  • Full system inspection and testing
  • New thermostats with programmable features
  • 12-month warranty on all parts and labor
  • Annual maintenance recommended

Roof System

  • Complete tear-off and replacement (not just repair)
  • Premium architectural shingles
  • New flashing and vent installations
  • Gutters and downspouts included
  • 10-year manufacturer warranty
  • Licensed roofing contractors only

Plumbing System

  • Copper pipes throughout the property
  • New fixtures and faucets
  • Updated water heater with warranty
  • Full system pressure testing
  • No leaks or drips guaranteed
  • Professional plumbing inspections

Electrical System

  • Modern electrical panel upgrade
  • GFCI outlets in all required areas
  • New interior and exterior switches
  • Updated lighting fixtures
  • Electrical inspection certificate
  • Complies with all building codes

Windows & Doors

  • All exterior doors replaced if needed
  • Updated interior doors (smooth operation)
  • New locks and hardware included
  • Weatherstripping applied throughout
  • Warranty on all door hardware
  • Ensure no drafts or gaps

Interior Finishes

  • Professional painting throughout
  • New appliances installed and tested
  • Cleaned and sanitized before occupancy
  • All carpet cleaned or replaced
  • Lights and switches replaced
  • Ceiling fans in appropriate rooms

Safety Standards

  • Smoke detectors in every bedroom
  • Carbon monoxide detector installed
  • Fire extinguishers provided
  • All safety equipment inspected
  • Emergency exit routes clear
  • Access to emergency services

Accessibility Features

  • Ramps and accessibility modifications available
  • Low-threshold entries where requested
  • Wide doorways in common areas
  • Accessible bathroom features
  • Accommodations for disabilities considered
  • Fair Housing accessibility compliance

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind our work. Every renovation includes comprehensive warranties and quality assurances.

Structural Warranty

Foundation, framing, and structural components are warranted for defects in materials and workmanship for up to 5 years.

Systems Warranty

HVAC, plumbing, and electrical systems carry 12-month warranties on both parts and labor.

Cosmetic Warranty

Paint, flooring, and cosmetic improvements are warranted for 6 months against defects.

Our Renovation Process

We believe in complete transparency in how we work. Here's exactly how we transform properties for our veterans and families.

1

Property Acquisition

Properties are carefully selected based on location, condition, and potential for veteran housing.

2

Assessment & Planning

Professional inspectors assess current condition and create detailed renovation plans with cost estimates.

3

Licensed Contractors

All work is performed by licensed, insured contractors experienced in residential renovations.

4

Quality Inspections

Independent inspections at multiple stages ensure work meets our standards and local building codes.

5

Final Certification

Properties receive final inspections and certificates before being made available to applicants.
